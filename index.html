About Us

A place with incredible natural beauty, wild rocky landscapes and a special historical interest. Meteora Park is an ideal destination for those who are thirsty for adventure as well as for those who want to escape from stress and everyday life, as the beauty of the landscape absorbs any negative thought and offers the serenity of the mind.

Nothing compares to the magic of the sunset at Meteora Park, as with sunset over the green hills, golden-red rays bathe the giant rocks of Meteora.


Here you will enjoy unique traditional dishes cooked on fire (BBQ) as well as local wines. Salads, potatoes, onions, cabbage, meat and more will be found in our restaurant

After you are full of the variety of meats, fresh salads and fruits, an open- air cinema will follow (James Bond on the Meteora) as well as a live DJ performance and traditional dances accompanied by live music, thus completing a unique unforgettable evening.

Activities: Mountain biking – hiking – climbing
